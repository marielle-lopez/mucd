Melbourne University Cheer and Dance Club

Who Are We?

The University of Melbourne Cheer and Dance Club is one of the univerisity's newest sports affiliated teams. As a representative of Melbourne University, our Cheer and Dance Club is one of Victoria's premier squads.

We pride ourselves on being a friendly and supportive club that achieves strong competition results through our dedication and passion.

Come Join!

The Melbourne University Cheer and Dance Club began in 2012 with only 24 members, and now offers cheerleading alongside the multiple dance styles of pom, lyrical, hip-hop, and jazz.

Come join us in 2024! You will not regret it! Our club offers a large range of teams, meaning that all potential athletes will be able to find a team that suits them!

History

In 2017, our All Star Pom team, MU Heat, not only received 1st place and Pom Grand Champions at DCE's Asia Pacific Grand Internationals, they also received a bid to complete at the Dance Worlds in Orlando, Florida in 2018! They went on to place 9th in the world, the only Australian All-Girl Open Pom team to place Top 10 in history!
